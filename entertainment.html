SOUND

When you purchase your Ford, one of the things you generally want to change are the speakers. Whether it be because you want a more juicy sound or just want to upgrade, it's always something us car owners tend to consider. Changing speakers can be a pain, it takes a lot of patience and also a lot of tools, but is so worth it in the end. Coverting your speakers to aftermarket ones can also be pricey, with speaker adapters having to be considered and if the car can witheld the new power, it's a lot of points which have to be considered when forking out the expenses.

There are multiple different aftermarket speakers you can go for which will give you a crazy sound, such as:

  • Alpine
  • In Phase
  • Juice
  • Pioneer
  • JVC
  • Focal
  • Sony
  • EDGE
  • VIBE
  • FLi

Through research, personal experience and other webpage reviews, there are two brands which stand out to us at 100% Ford and would recommend to you for upgrading your Ford's speakers.
