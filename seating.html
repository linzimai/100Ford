SEATING

When you see an ST, RS or Mustang, you never see standard seats- its Recaro's. These are unique to Fords design and one of Ford's best features to their vehicles. The Recaro's are installed to reproduce that OEM look and give the vehicle that sport feel. If you do not have these seats in your vehicle, they can be installed, however the seat braces will have to redesigned and installed into your Ford to achieve the look; meaning it will be a lot of work and take a lot of sweat and tears. There are various Ford's which these Recaro's are already preinstalled into, such as:

  • Escort RS Turbo, RS Cosworth, RS2000
  • Ford Escort XR3
  • Fiesta RS Turbo, RS1800i
  • Fiesta MK7 ST
  • Ford Puma Millennium Special Edition
  • Ford Sierra
  • Focus Mk1 ST170 & SVT
  • Focus Mk2 ST & RS
  • Focus Mk3 ST & RS
  • Mondeo ST200 and ST220
  • 1979 Mustang Indy Pace Car
  • 2000 Mustang SVT Cobra R
  • Ford Shelby Cobra GT 500
  • Ford Mustang Boss 302
  • Ford Mustang Shelby GT350/GT350R
